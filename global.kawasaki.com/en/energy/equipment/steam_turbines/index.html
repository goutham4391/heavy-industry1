Industrial Steam Turbines

Kawasaki's Industrial Steam Turbine have met many custormers' needs for the power generation use mainly.
Since 1956, Kawasaki has used its long and extensive turbine experience to expand its manufacturing record upto 340 units amounting to 4,800 MW.

Features

  • Originally own developed technology and manufacturing
  • High Reliability and sufficient experience
  • High efficiency and performance
  • Excellent maintenance service

Line-up

1) Condensing Steam Turbine

  • Exhaust steam is cooled and returned to the water by the condenser.
  • Extraction steam system (using steam from intermediate stage of Steam Turbine) can be designed.
  • For generator coupling, Reduction-gear coupled type is applied to small capacity model, and Direct coupled type is applied to middle and large capacity model.

2) Back-Pressure Steam Turbine

  • Exhaust steam can be used for process steam of factory heat demand.
  • Extraction steam system (using steam from intermediate stage of Steam Turbine) can be designed.
  • For generator coupling, Reduction-gear coupled type is applied to small capacity model, and Direct coupled type is applied to middle and large capacity model.

Appricable Specification

  • Inlet Steam Condition (Steam Pressure / Temperature) :0.2MPaG / Sat. ~ 14MPaG / 570℃
  • Power Output :~150MW / unit

Kawasaki's Industrial Steam Turbine can apply many steam conditions, and meet customers' demand and / or request of electricity and heat demand.
